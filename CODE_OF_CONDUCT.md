# Code of Conduct

We are building infrastructure that people will trust with their data. Trust begins with how we treat each other.

## Our standards

- Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Accept well-intentioned critique.
- Focus on the problem, not the person.
- Document decisions and admit uncertainty.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the core maintainers at conduct@mimir-mesh.dev. We will investigate and respond privately.
